Stranger Things

A Netflix Original Series

Overview

Stranger Things is a sci-fi horror TV series set in the 1980s in the small town of Hawkins, Indiana. The story begins with the mysterious disappearance of a young boy and the appearance of a strange girl with supernatural powers. As they uncover secret experiments and a terrifying alternate dimension called the Upside Down, the characters are drawn into a dark, thrilling mystery.

Key Information

Genre: Science Fiction, Horror, Mystery

Created by: The Duffer Brothers

First released: July 15, 2016

Platform: Netflix

Cast & Creative Team

  • Millie Bobby Brown as Eleven
  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • Gaten Matarazzo, Caleb McLaughlin, Natalia Dyer, Charlie Heaton, Joe Keery
  • Creators: Matt and Ross Duffer

Main Characters

Eleven: A girl with psychokinetic powers who escapes from a government lab.

Mike: A smart, caring boy who becomes close to Eleven.

Will: A boy who goes missing and becomes central to the Upside Down mystery.

Hopper: The Hawkins police chief who investigates the strange events.

Reviews

IGN: 9/10 - “A love letter to 80s horror and sci-fi.”
